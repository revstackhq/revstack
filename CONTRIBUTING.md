# Contributing to Revstack

> [!WARNING]
> **Contribution Lock Active**
>
> Thank you for your interest in contributing to Revstack! We are thrilled to have the community's support. However, at this time, we are **not accepting external Pull Requests**.

### Why the contribution lock?

Our Provider Development Kit (PDK) lifecycle, abstract core interfaces, internal APIs, and strict versioning systems are currently in heavy flux and being actively designed. We want to prevent the community from wasting time building PRs that we will inevitably have to reject or heavily refactor due to internal architectural shifts.

### What happens to Pull Requests?

Any Pull Requests opened during this phase will be **automatically closed without review**.

### How you can still help

We absolutely still want your feedback! Please feel free to open **Issues** for:

- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Architectural discussions

We plan to lift this contribution lock and open up for external contributions once the foundation is rock-solid. Thank you for your patience and understanding!
